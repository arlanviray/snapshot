The Magic of Storytelling
Combining the wonder of AR with the magic of storytelling, Snapchat has collaborated with Disney at the 2023 Cannes Lions International Festival of Creativity in an event which has garnered considerable coverage.
Furthermore, it has helped to create Disney Wonder of Friendship: The Experience in Berlin, an immersive multi-sensory pop-up exhibition in the German capital.
These unique experiences gave the public the opportunity to engage with classic tales through contemporary technology - something Fintan Gillespie, our Director of Large Client Solutions, explored in more detail in the Performance Marketing Unlocked podcast.

Lens Folders Have Gone Live
To facilitate easier collaboration between businesses and external Lens creators, we have created a Lens Folder within Business Manager.
This means it's no longer necessary to submit Lenses across multiple ad accounts. Instead, they can be pushed directly to the Lens Folder for organic or sponsored use!

In the News
Mind Stories in AR
In collaboration with Snapchat, the FreeTech Academy, an arm of the Axel Springer Academy of Journalism and Technology, has introduced an AR Lens focused on mental health.
Created as part of the think tank's Mind Stories project, the Lens guides users through a breathing exercise which can be done alongside the user's Snapchat contacts, while their multi-part show provides a platform for young people to inspire and support their peers.
